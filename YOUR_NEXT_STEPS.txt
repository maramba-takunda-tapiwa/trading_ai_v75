Enter these fields:
- **App ID:** Your Saxo application ID

### Save

Click the big **"Save Credentials"** button.

Wait for the green checkmark: **"‚úÖ Credentials saved successfully!"**

‚úÖ If you see the green message, move to Step 3.

**‚ùå If you see an error:**

## STEP 3: Deploy (1-2 minutes)

### Go Back to Overview Tab

Click the **"Overview"** tab at the top.

Find the big green **"Deploy"** button in the "Controls" section.

Click it.

### Confirm Dialog

Click **"Deploy"** to confirm.

### Wait

The dashboard will:
1. Show "DEPLOYING..." for a moment
2. Change to **"Deployment Status: DEPLOYED"**
3. Start updating metrics every 5 seconds

‚úÖ If you see "DEPLOYED", you're live! Move to Step 4.

## STEP 4: Watch It Trade (Automatic)

### What Happens Next (Automatic, No Action Needed)

1. **Trader launches** in background
2. **Connects to Saxo** demo account
3. **Downloads data** (EUR/USD hourly)
4. **Analyzes market** looking for breakouts
5. **Executes trades** when conditions met
6. **Logs each trade** to CSV
7. **Updates dashboard** in real-time

### Where to Look

**Trades Tab:**
- First trade usually appears within 30-120 minutes
- You'll see columns: Time, Entry, Exit, R, P&L, Outcome, Equity

**Charts Tab:**
- Equity curve shows as line
- Updates after each trade closes

**Overview Tab:**
- Metrics update every 5 seconds
- Watch: Win Rate, Max DD, Profit Factor

### What to Expect (72 Hours)

- **Hour 1:** Trader analyzing, possibly waiting for setup
- **Hour 4:** Usually 1-2 trades
- **Hour 12:** Usually 5-10 trades
- **Hour 24:** Usually 10-20 trades
- **Hour 72:** Usually 15-30 trades total, validate strategy

## What If...

### "I don't have Saxo credentials yet"
- Go to https://www.saxobank.com/
- Sign up for demo account
- Get your App ID and Token
- Then come back and add them

### "Trades aren't appearing after 1 hour"
- Check "Alerts" at top of dashboard (red warnings)
- Check Settings - are credentials still saved?
- Try clicking "Refresh" button
- Check logs: `docker-compose logs trading-ai`

### "I want to stop it"
- Click the **"Stop"** button (red, in Controls)
- Or in terminal: `docker-compose down`

### "I want to restart"
- Click **"Deploy"** again
- Or in terminal: `docker-compose up -d`

### "Everything seems broken"
- Restart Docker:
  ```powershell
  docker-compose down
  docker-compose up -d
  ```
- Wait 5 seconds
- Reload http://localhost
